Frequently Asked Questions

 

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a gathering of Makers, Hackers, Coders and Enthusiasts to compete in a software based competition to develop the best project.

When and Where?

GetDigitl Hackathon is on the 20th & 21st August 9am - 4pm.

Hosted at Google Campus London.

Who can join in?

Anyone can join in if you're over the age of 10! However under 14s require a parent present.

Is the event free?

Yup! We won't charge a penny, however any donations are accepted.

I've Never coded before in my life! What do I do?

That's okay! Our competition is aimed at more competant coders, but feel free to come along, learn, join in and meet new people!

Will there be food?

Absolutely! Lunch will be provided both days and breakfast on the second day, and we will cater to any dietry requirements if you let us know before hand.

I am a Digital artist, am I welcome?

Of course! We suggest you team up with someone with coding experience.

I only know one language? AND IT'S FORTRAN! What do?

That's fine! You can code in any language, we will only be judging the end result.

Where do I park my Sinclair c5 and similar vehicles?

As cool as that is, we recommend you take the tube or bus because we are right inside the congestion charge zone. Feel free to bring pictures though.

4 wheels are overrated. Where can I park my bike?

There should be secure bike racks on site. If not you can bring it inside.

I get lonely. Can I work in a team?

Teams are encouraged however you can register as an individual.

What sort of prizes are we giving out?

You'll have to wait and see ;)

What child safety measures do we have in place?

All our adult organisers are DBS checked. You can review our Child protection policy here.

How can I get involved?

You can register to compete here.

Can I sponsor this event?

Absolutely. See our sponsorship page.

Something else?
