Sponsoring the GetDigitl Hackathons

Companies, Parties or individuals can sponsor any GetDigitl event.

What can I give?

You can give prizes, money, swag, merchandise or anything that you think could be helpful.

What will I get in return?

Our policy is to advertise you or your company depending on the value of the sponsor, no matter the form of sponsorship. We can not always offer the same amount of advertisement, so please contact us! In general we offer your Logo on either/all of: our Website; Social Media; T-shirts; Event banners; Workshops at event; Sell/advertise at event; Judge the event.   To get in touch please contact us!
